Contact Information

Furrific is operated by a team is made up of passionate members who dedicate their free time and energy throughout the year.

Got a question, idea, encountered a problem, or just want to get in touch? Feel free to reach out using the contact details listed below. You can contact us by email or Telegram.

Not sure who to contact? Just drop us a message through to General Enquiries, and we’ll make sure it gets to the right place.

NB. Messages via Telegram are answered by a member of the appropriate team and are not an AI backed service.
