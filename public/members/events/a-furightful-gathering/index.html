Welcome to the Club

If you're reading this it means you've likely received an invite to join us in a new adventure. Furrific isn't just another convention but a members-only club. Membership is deliberately limited to retain an old-school, small convention vibe in which you can walk into a room and feel surrounded by friends from afar.

The club is kicking off with its first event at the Seacote Hotel in St. Bees. We'll have a full line up of events just as you'd expect with a small convention. It won't be too heavy and there'll be plenty of time to simply relax and socialise.

Every member is invited to be involved as much or little as they would like, all contributions are welcome.

As a members club all new members must be invited by two existing members that act as their referees before being approved by committee. If we no longer have capacity remaining at our events then new membership approvals will be held until the club's members approve expansion.

You might've noticed a small detail there, about the club members approving something. The club is fully democratic; every year a committee is elected to steer the club but large, sweeping changes are voted on by all members at an AGM (Annual General Meeting). That means, as a member, you directly decide who you want to lead the club and vote on the big decisions.

But it's not just about hotels & meetings, as a club we aim to eventually bring additional benefits such as web & email hosting, skills sharing & workshops and more…

Membership is just £5 per year whether you attend an event or not. For the first event the membership fee is included in the event ticket. If you’ve been invited but can’t attend our first event but would still like to join the club or future events you can still register just for membership.

Venue

Seacote Hotel, St. Bees, West Cumbria 31st October 2025

We have secured use of the Seacote Hotel in St. Bees. This is a traditional, large seaside hotel without the corporate vibe of modern conference facilities. They’ve got a large event space with bar & catering for us to have a great time in, as well as separate bar & restaurant areas you may use if you just need to get away from things for a bit.

The hotel is situated next to St. Bees beach which is fantastic for taking in the sea air or just going for a stroll. There’s ample car parking for everyone, and the nearest train station is just a 10-minute walk away.

Bedrooms

We’ve a variety of options for your stay which include single, double, twin, triple, and quad rooms. All are available on a first-come, first-served basis. If we’re unable to provide you with your first choice of room we will try to offer alternatives.

All rooms include en-suite facilities, Wi-Fi, TVs, towels & linen, and tea & coffee making facilities.

Due to the nature of the hotel all of the rooms vary a little and aren’t cookie cutter copy & paste like chain hotels. But we hope you’ll agree that they all represent excellent value for money.

Food

All meals from Friday evening until Monday morning are included in your stay. We’ll be providing a lunch & dinner buffet as well as breakfast. Dining options in the local area are limited so we’re sure you’ll appreciate not going hungry! Vegan & gluten-free options will also be provided but do be sure to let us know if you have any specific requirements so we can ensure you are catered for.

Cost

All prices are per-person based on a 3-night stay. Prices are based on maximum occupancy of the room. Prices include event entry, bed, breakfast (Saturday, Sunday, and Monday), lunch buffet (Saturday & Sunday), and dinner buffet (Friday, Saturday, and Sunday). - Single room: £350 - Double / Twin room: £260 - Triple room (1 double, 1 single): £240 - Quad room (1 double, 2 singles): £220

Payment will be required either by April 6th or two weeks after your registration is approved, whichever is the later. If you need to delay payment please let us know and we’ll try to accommodate your needs as best as we’re able.

Additional nights

Need to arrive early or depart late? That can be arranged too. Please keep in mind that these nights are provided for convenience or to enable you to do some local sight seeing. All prices are based on maximum occupancy of rooms. The cost per-person of rooms will increase if beds are unoccupied. Prices are for bed & breakfast only; there will be no convention events operating outside of the Friday to Sunday period.

Room partners

Want to share with someone that isn’t a member? You can invite them to join too! If you’re not sure who you’ll be sharing with yet, don’t worry; you don’t need to make an immediate decision. Once your membership is approved you can contact other members through our Telegram group to see if there’s anyone interested in being a room mate.

Entertainments

Although we don’t have the space & facilities to provide an event to the same scale as NFC or CFz we will ensure the events are entertaining and provide you with lasting memories (hopefully for the right reasons). During the weekend there are plans afoot for the following:

  • Casino games (no money involved)
  • Pub quiz
  • Pumpkin carving competition
  • Karaoke & dance nights
  • Fursuit games
  • Movie showing
  • Boardgame spaces
  • Photography opportunities
  • Special interest panels

Dealers den

We will evaluate demand for this during registration. If one occurs it will be an opportunity for dealers to trade for a couple of hours during the daytime. Our members site will also contain a directory of dealers that are members of the club.

Fursuit lounge

The lounge area will be a simple side room from the primary event space providing fans, water & light snacks.

Even more...

Members will be invited to run their own panels or stage performances.

Getting involved

Once your membership is approved we’ll provide you with invites into our Telegram group so you can meet other members.

Further questions

If there’s anything else you’d like to know please get in touch, your primary inviter will either be able to answer your questions or pass them on to the committee to address.
